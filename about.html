About

An Eye for Tradition

Kevin has been a expert about food and wine for decades. With an extensive career in luxury hotels, management, and real estate development, he has a deep passion for the art of celebration. In 1995, Kevin was invited by Master Sabreur, Robert Gourdin, to join the exclusive Club des Sabreurs. The Club, based in North America, featured Julia Child as their first member and was populated with the top hoteliers and restaurateurs in this continent.
